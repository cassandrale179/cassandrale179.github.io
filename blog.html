On Bojack Horseman

The most enlightened, depressing cartoon I've ever watched

Published: 17 December 2018 | tags: bookreviews

"Closure is a made up thing by Steven Spielberg to sell movie tickets. It, like true love and the Munich Olympics, doesn’t exist in the real world. The only thing to do now is just to keep living forward."

I thought Rick and Morty, created by the legendary Dan Harmon himself, was pretty depressing for an animated series. But Bojack Horseman had to steal the award for the feel-bad cartoon of its time. Accompanied with satirical dark humor, this is a mastery composite of critiques on society, the superficiality of Hollywood, and the unending escape of the ego.
